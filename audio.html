How It Works

How to make sure the audio of your project is captured optimally.

Types of production

We should go through the nature of your project and determine what types of shots we will need to film. Narrative vs documentary? Run-and-gun vs formal interviews? Corporate vs journalism?

Budget

We want to use the best equipment possible, but professional equipment can be costly. We can discuss if additional equipment is needed to address the requirements of your project without going over budget. This is also when clear expectations should be set.

The Shoot

Working closely with you and your onscreen talent will not only give us the best chance to get great audio, but also to ensure everyone will have a smooth working experience.

Post Production Support

I keep our audio files for as long as my hard drives allow, you can be assured that there will also be a backup. If needed, I can process / tinker the audio files to fit your post workflow.
