How It Works

We will take you through every step of the process

Initial Meeting

We will sit down and have a conversation with you about what you like, what your taste in films and music is, etc, so we have an idea what how to film and edit your wedding video.

Before the big day

Plans change. You are busy planning and prepping for your day. Just let us know whenever something changes and we'll adjust accordingly.

Wedding Day

We'll arrive nice and early to get our equipment setup. Don't worry about us. You enjoy yourself and you'll do our thing!

Editing

While you enjoy your honeymoon (or go back to work, as many newlyweds actually end up doing), we'll get busy nerding out in front of the computer editing your video. We go back and forth on our edits until you are 100% satisfied with our work.
